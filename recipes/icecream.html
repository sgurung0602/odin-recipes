Ice Cream

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Salt
  • Egg Yolks
  • Chocolate
  • Heavy Cream
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Bring the milk, sugar, cocoa, and salt to a simmer in a saucepan.
  2. Whisk Â½ of the milk mixture into the yolks, return to the pan, and stir until thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chopped chocolate.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Refrigerate in a chilled bowl until cold.
  6. Add the remaining ingredients, then freeze in an ice cream maker.
